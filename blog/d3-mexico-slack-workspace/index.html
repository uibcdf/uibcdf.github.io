D3 Mexico Slack Workspace

2017-10-23 16:28

Also available in:

Español

Together with some students from the UNAM postgraduate course "From the molecular target's structure to the rational drug design - 2018", we have created a new slack workspace to share experiences on the field of the Drug Design and Discovery in México. We will work to make this team an important meeting point where mexican researchers can find useful information and interactions fostering community consolidation.

Drug Design and Discovery México - Slack Team

Get in contact with us if you want to join us.
